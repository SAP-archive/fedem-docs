Rack and Pinion

The Rack-and-Pinion object is used to represent a constraint between the translational motion of a Prismatic Joint and the rotation in a Revolute Joints in the FEDEM model.

The figure above illustrates the Rack-and-Pinion properties, which only consist of the Transmission output ratio. This is the relative translational velocity of the output prismatic joint with respect to the velocity of the input revolute joint.
