Origin tab

The figure above illustrates the Origin properties of the joint, which consist of the following items:

Name:Description:
Position This frame displays data for the translational part of the joint location.
OrientationThis frame displays data for the rotational part of the joint location.
Relative positioning of TriadsThese two toggles control whether the master and/or slave triad will be moved along with the joint itself, if the joint is moved.

The fields of the Position and Orientation frames have similar interpretation as in the Origin tab of the Triad property panel. Together they define the joint coordinate system of the selected joint.
