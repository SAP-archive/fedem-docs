Tower

The Tower object is a sub-assembly used to represent the turbine tower in a wind turbine model.

The figure above illustrates the Tower properties, which consist of the following items:

Name:Description:
MassDisplays the total mass of the tower.
Center of GravityDisplays the location of the tower's center of gravity, in either Global coordinates or in Local coordinates with respect to the origin of the tower assembly.
Visualize 3DEnables or disables 3D visualization of all the Beam elements the tower consists of.

The default Tower representation in FEDEM consists of a single Generic Part, and a Triad in each end of the tower. A tower model may have to be a bit more complex than that, which is why we offer additional approaches for modeling the tower. Please see the chapter on "Wind Power Modelling" in the FEDEM User's Guide for details on these approaches.

Note: A valid wind turbine model is not required to contain a separate Tower object. The tower may optionally be an integrated part of the underlying support structure. It may then be modelled manually, by importing a FE Part or through other means.

Note: The Visualize 3D toggle has a tri-state value. In addition to the usual ON and OFF states, it can also have a neither-ON-nor-OFF state, in which the corresponding ON/OFF settings on each underlying beam element are applied instead. This is usually the default setting for such tri-state toggles.
