Nacelle

The Nacelle object is a sub-assembly used to represent the nacelle in a wind turbine model.

The figure above illustrates the Nacelle properties, which consist of the following items:

Name:Description:
MassDisplays the total mass of the rotor.
Center of GravityDisplays the location of the rotor's center of gravity, in either Global coordinates or in Local coordinates with respect to the origin of the rotor assembly.

The Nacelle sub-assembly typically contains many sub-objects in its model tree list. It may contain one or two Shaft assemblies, depending on whether it has a direct driveline or a geared one. In the latter case, the Nacelle sub-assembly also has a Gearbox assembly among its sub-objects. It also have one Generator assembly. In addition, it normally contains a Nacelle Part, one or two Bearing Revolute Joints and some Triads.
