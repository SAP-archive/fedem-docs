Gearbox

The Gearbox object is a sub-assembly used to represent a gearbox in a wind turbine model.

The figure above illustrates the Gearbox properties, which consist of the following items:

Name:Description:
MassDisplays the total mass of the gearbox.
Center of GravityDisplays the location of the gearbox's center of gravity, in either Global coordinates or in Local coordinates with respect to the origin of the gearbox assembly.

The Gearbox sub-assembly normally contains objects of type Gear, Revolute Joint and Triad in its model tree list.
