Material

The Material object is used to describe material properties of beam elements (linear isotropic material law only). They are referred to via the Beam cross section object.

The figure above illustrates the Material properties, which consist of the following fields:

Name:Description:
pMass density.
EYoung’s modulus of elasticity.
νPoisson’s ratio.
GShear modulus of elasticity.

Only one of the parameters ν and G needs to be specified, as they are connected through the relation G = E/(2+2ν). Whenever you change one of these values, the other value will update automatically.
