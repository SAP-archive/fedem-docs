Results tab

The figure above shows the Results properties of the joint. It contains toggles for activating output of certain solution variables related to the joint, such that the quantities are available for curve plotting, etc. The Spring variables and Damper variables frames will appear only if the joint contains at least one Spring-Damper constrained DOF.

In the Joint variables frame, the following toggles are available:

Name:Description:
DeflectionOutput deflection in each joint DOF.
VelocityOutput velocity in each joint DOF.
AccelerationOutput acceleration in each joint DOF.
Friction/Reaction forceOutput either the friction force or the reaction for in each joint DOF, depending on the Constraint Type (see below).
Friction EnergyOutput friction energy in each joint DOF which have a Friction associated with it.

In the Spring variables frame, the following toggles are available. They will apply to all spring-damper constrained DOFs in the joint:

Name:Description:
StiffnessOuput the stiffness of the joint DOF springs.
LengthOutput the length of the joint DOF springs.
DeflectionOutput deflection in the joint DOF springs.
ForceOutput the joint DOF spring forces.
EnergiesOutput the energy stored in the joint DOF springs.

In the Damper variables frame, the following toggles are available. Also they will apply to all spring-damper constrained DOFs in the joint:

Name:Description:
CoefficientOutput the damping coefficient in the joint DOFs.
LengthOutput the length of the joint DOF dampers.
VelocityOutput the velocity in the joint DOF damper.
ForceOutput the joint DOF damping forces.
EnergiesOutput the energy stored in the joint DOF dampers.

The Friction/Reaction force toggle will activate output of friction forces in each joint DOF which is either Free or Spring-Damper constrained, and have a Friction associated with it. If the joint DOF is either Fixed or Prescribed, the same toggle will activate output of the associated reaction force instead.
