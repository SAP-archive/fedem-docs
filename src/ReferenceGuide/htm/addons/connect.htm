Connect

Connect is an assistive tool to simplify the process of connecting triads, nodes on parts, and ground with a joint.

The figure below illustrates a model with two FE-parts (NAS-files). Both parts have spiders, with a connectable node in the same position. To connect these nodes with a joint:

  1. Start "Connect" from the Addons-menu in FEDEM.
  2. Click the position in the FEDEM model that has two or more nodes.
    The figure illustrates the current state of the app.
  3. You can now click to select what will be master (M) and slave (S) of the joint. "Master <-> Slave" will swap master and slave.
  4. Click "Create Joint" to create the joint. The joint will be fixed by default. You can change the properties of the joint in FEDEM.
