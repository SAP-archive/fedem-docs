Directional Fatigue Factor (DFF)

In the case when an analysis is done with unidirectional waves, but the true wave spectrum is distributed over many directions, the calculated life will be less than true life, because damage will be distributed over the pipe cross section.

A simplified approach to take this into account is to multiply the resulting fatigue with a directional factor (DFF) >1. This app calculates a DFF based on given directional distribution of waves. The calculation presumes the distribution of Hs to be the same for each direction.
