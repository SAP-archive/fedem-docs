Graphics Modeller

The Graphics Modeller is used to interactively view, select and modify objects.

You can navigate the 3D model view with the following main operations:

Operation:        Description:
Pan viewPress and hold the F1 key on the keyboard, while moving the mouse over the view to pan the view in the x/y-plane of the view.
Zoom viewPress and hold the F2 key on the keyboard, while moving the mouse over the view to zoom the view along the z-axis of the view. You can alternatively roll the middle mouse button to zoom as well. The Z-key will also zoom the view.
Rotate viewPress and hold the middle mouse button. If you do this on the center part of the view, it will rotate the view around the x- and y-axis of the view. If you do this at the edge of the view, it will rotate around the z-axis of the view. You can alternatively press and hold the F3 key (instead of the middle mouse button). Further, you can also rotate the view by using the arrow keys, in combinaton with the Alt key for rotation around the z-axis.
Center on objectPress and hold F4 while clicking on an object to select and zoom to that object. This will set the rotation center for the view.

Snapping

When selecting objects in your model, the selection automatically snaps to a point on the object such as the nearest node on an FE part, the center point of a joint, and so on. This makes your selection quick and accurate.

Multiple selection

Some FEDEM commands, such as Smart Move and Delete, allow you to select several items at once. To select more than one item, press and hold down the Ctrl key and then click the items you want to add to your selection.

Selection history

FEDEM maintains a history of the items you select in the current session. This history can be accessed using the Select Backward and Select Forward commands.

Co-located items

Sometimes several items in a model are located very close together or on top of each other; in FEDEM, these are called co-located items. To select a co-located item, click the same spot several times to cycle through the items. FEDEM cycles from the item closest to the viewer to the one furthest from the viewer.
