System Requirements

The following are minimum system/hardware requirements and recommended settings for optimal FEDEM performance.

Name:Description:
Processor Recommended: Pentium 4 @ 2 GHz
Minimum: Pentium III @ 850 MHz
RAM Recommended: 2 GB
Minimum: 256 MB
Free disk space Dependent upon the size and complexity of mechanism models.
Larger models may require several GBs.
Graphics card Recommended: high end graphics card
Required: OpenGL® API support
Pointing device Three-button mouse with wheel.
Resolution setting 1,280 x 1,024 pixels or higher.
